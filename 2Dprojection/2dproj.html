Projection of u on v

Let u and v be vectors in a vector space. The projection of u onto v is given by the following formula: \[ \text{proj}_u(v) = \frac{\mathbf{u}.\mathbf{v}}{||\mathbf{v}||^2} \mathbf{v} \] One way to intuitively understand this is to imagine there is a light source that casts a shadow of u on v. The light source's rays are perpendicular to v.
